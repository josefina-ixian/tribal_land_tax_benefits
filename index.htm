Nuclear Deployment on Tribal Lands

Analyzing the Inflation Reduction Act opportunity for Clean Firm Generation and Sovereign Energy Independence.

What are the Financial Benefits?

30% Base ITC Available via Elective (Direct) Pay for tax-exempt Tribes if wage/apprenticeship rules are met.
+10% Energy Community Bonus Applies to most Tribal lands formerly reliant on fossil fuel industries.
+10% Domestic Content Bonus Additional credit for using US-manufactured nuclear components.

The Process to Get the Benefit

1

IRS Registration: Use the IRS Energy Credits Online portal to register the facility *before* filing.

2

Placed in Service: Complete construction and commission the reactor.

3

Claim Refund: File Form 990-T and Form 3800. The IRS sends a cash payment directly to the Tribe.
