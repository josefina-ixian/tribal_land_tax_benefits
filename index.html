Compliance & Timing Requirements

📝

§48E ITC + Direct Pay Registration

Required (IRS ECO portal). Pre-filing registration before placed-in-service. No competitive component for base credit.

⏳

§48E(h) Indian Land Bonus

Required — 2026 window: Feb 2 – Aug 7, 2026. Competitive allocation. 200 MW carve-out for Indian Land Cat. 2 in 2026.

🛡️

FEOC Restrictions

Foreign Entity of Concern (FEOC) rules apply to projects starting construction in 2026+. Components from Russia/China may disqualify credits.

Executive Takeaways (v2 Research)

  • ● **Microreactor Optimization:** Projects < 5MW can capture up to 70% in cash refunds by stacking LIC and Indian Land bonuses.
  • ● **SMR/Large Economics:** While ineligible for the Indian Land adder, utility-scale nuclear still averages 50% support through DC and EC adders.
  • ● **Uranium Legacy:** Sites with historic uranium activity may pre-qualify as Energy Community Brownfields, adding 10% to the ITC stack automatically.
  • ● **OBBBA Phasedown:** Full credit value is maintained through 2033 for nuclear, a significantly longer window than wind/solar under current legislation.
